  • Turn off power at the breaker before inspection

  • Use insulated tools when working with electricity

Emergency Situations

If you experience any of these, turn off power immediately and call for help:

  • Burning smell or smoke
  • Visible sparks or arcing
  • Hot outlets or panels
  • Electric shocks
